3PM FOR SNICKERS
SNICKERS
BACKGROUND

The 'YNYWHY' campaign has delivered 12 years of success for Snickers. It's such an enduring icon

because it's based on human truth – an empty stomach can instantly transform how people act.

This insight informed a global strategy that has been expertly adapted to different markets all

around the world by tapping into local cultural insights and messaging.

PROBLEM & CHALLENGE

Most Snickers advertisements in the past were TVC type. In order to adapt to the trend of the

Internet era, Snickers wants to launch more promotional campaigns placed on social medias or

website platforms.

TARGET AUDIENCE
Aussies in need of a 3pm pick-me-up.
INSIGHT

A natural response to circadian rhythms usually leaves us short of energy at 3pm.

Whether an office worker or a student,

they all need a Snickers at 3pm to refuel to work/ study more efficiently.

BIG IDEA

It's 3pm, time to get SNICKERS!

EXECUTION 1: '3PM FOR SNICKERS' POSTER & BANNER

We cleverly combined Snickers with a clock to spell out '3pm', trying to tell our audience that

it 's 3pm, time to refuel through Snickers to stop to make hunger blunders.

And the posters and banners will be placed on various social platforms at 3pm in conjunction with

our next execution.

EXECUTION 2: IT'S TIME FOR SNICKERS!

We will work with some of well-known social apps, and PUSH POP-UPS will be released to users at

the same time at 3 pm, reading 'Get snickers, and stop to make hunger blunders!'.

It emphasises the big idea of our campaign that it's time to refuel with our Snickers at 3pm. And if

users clicked on the push, they would find posters/banners/advertising placements about

#3pmForSnickers posted by our official account on various social media platforms.

EXECUTION 3: SNICKERS 3PM
(LIMITED TIKTOK STICKER)

Snickers will be collaborating with Tiktok to create a Snickers Sticker to be placed on Tiktok. This

sticker will be available for a limited time from 3:00-3:05 each day.

STICKER IDEA: At first there will be a black eye effect under the users' eyes and a snickers

appearing around the mouth. The word 'Open Mouth' appears. When the users opens their mouth,

SNICKERS will appear around their mouth. Each time users open their mouth, they 'eat' some of

the Snickers. Once all the Snickers have been 'eaten', the black eye effect disappears.

This sticker is designed to show that eating a Snickers at 3pm will help to eliminate negative

emotions such as tiredness and hunger to stop to make hunger blunders.

LEAVE AN 'EASTER EGG' FOR USERS !

The Snickers official account does not actively promote the sticker, but will repost videos posted

by users after they have spotted the sticker and filmed it for posting.

EXAMPLE OF FORWARDING COPY: Congratulations on your discovery of this hidden Snickers

Sticker! Feel asleep? Can't chill? Maybe you just need a SNICKERS! #Snickers #3pmForSnickers
