MAKE YOUR LIFE DIFFERENT
VIC GOV_FOSTER CARE AGENCY
BACKGROUND

The number of children needing a foster home is increasing, but the number of individuals willing

to foster a child is decreasing. Besides, Victorians know little about foster care other than it is

difficult and strenuous.

PROBLEM & CHALLENGE

HOW could the foster care agency shape the understanding of and appreciation

for foster care within the broader community,

and HOW to get people who are hesitant to adopt to join the ranks of foster cares.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Our audiences are Victorians aged 18 to 24 and aged 35 to 39 who desire to become a foster care.

They may be young couples who are physically unable to have children, the LGBTQIA+ community

who are unable to have children for biological reasons, or single youth.

INSIGHT

Many young people hesitant to become a foster carer, because they know little about the

happiness of having their own children.

BIG IDEA

Children can reduce your loneliness and add color and vitality to your lives.

We encourage young people to discover all the good things that foster children can bring to their

lives while trying to learn more about foster care. If you are willing to foster a kid, more color and

energy maybe will be added into your life.
