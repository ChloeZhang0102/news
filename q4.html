BETTER LIFE WITH LEAF
NISSAN
BACKGROUND

The Nissan Leaf is a compact 5-door hatchback pure electric vehicle developed and produced by

Nissan. As a pure electric vehicle, Nissan Leaf has zero exhaust and zero greenhouse gas

emissions when driving, which is environmentally friendly. And its range after charging has been

continuously improved, from 117km to 385km. Until December 2019, Nissan Leaf was listed as the

best- selling plug-in electric car in the world's history.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Urban professionals (perhaps with children) who commute mainly in metropolitan areas

are more concerned about the environment,

especially the impact of burning fossil fuels on global warming and pollution.

PROBLEM & CHALLENGE

The price is relatively high in the same category of cars,

and charging outside the metropolis may be inconvenient.

So our challenge is to make Leaf give full play to its advantages

to suit its target audience and then enter their shopping list.

INSIGHT

More and more attention has been paid to the problems caused by the combustion of fossil fuels

recently (such as global warming, environmental pollution, etc.).

And people have begun to consider environmental protection factors in choosing their

transportation tools (especially young people commuting in cities).

BIG IDEA

More LEAFs, More Green, Better Life.

Leaf is a cost-effective new energy vehicle with 0 exhaust emissions. It is friendly to the

environment and can reduce a certain degree of environmental pollution and bring a more

comfortable and better life to urban commuters.
