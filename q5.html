BACKGROUND

Death to Stock (DTS) is an Artist Owned Co-op that provides you with the freshest authentic stock

photos and videos; with unlimited downloads and new work added every month. We believe

everyone can be a creator because being an artist is an approach to your craft rather than a title

you acquire.
